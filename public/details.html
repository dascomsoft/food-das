GRILLED MEAT WITH SPICES


MEAT

Meat from Agro is rich in essential nutrients, including high-quality proteins, iron, zinc, and vitamins B12 and B6. Proteins help with tissue growth and repair, while iron aids in red blood cell production, improving oxygen circulation in the body. Zinc supports the immune system, and vitamins B12 and B6 are crucial for energy production and brain function. Through sustainable farming methods, Agro ensures nutritious products that benefit the body while being environmentally responsible.

SPICES

Agro's spices are rich in antioxidants, protecting cells from free radical damage. Many, like turmeric and ginger, have anti-inflammatory properties that support immune health. They also aid digestion and provide essential micronutrients like iron, magnesium, and vitamins.

SALAD


PEAR

Pears are a good source of dietary fiber, aiding digestion and promoting gut health. They are rich in antioxidants like vitamin C, which supports the immune system. Pears are also low in calories and provide hydration due to their high water content.

TOMATO

Tomatoes are packed with vitamins A and C, supporting vision and immune function. They contain lycopene, a powerful antioxidant that helps reduce the risk of chronic diseases. Tomatoes are also low in calories and high in hydration.

ONION

Onions are rich in antioxidants like quercetin, which has anti-inflammatory properties. They support heart health by reducing cholesterol levels and improving blood circulation. Onions also contain vitamin C and fiber, aiding immunity and digestion.

PEAS

Peas are high in plant-based protein and fiber, supporting muscle growth and digestive health. They are rich in vitamins A, C, and K, promoting skin health and bone strength. Peas also provide antioxidants, which help fight inflammation.

Rice

Rice, especially brown rice, is a good source of complex carbohydrates, providing long-lasting energy. It contains important minerals like magnesium and phosphorus, which support bone health. Rice is also gluten-free and easy to digest.

A dish with pears, tomatoes, onions, peas, and rice provides a balanced mix of fiber, antioxidants, vitamins, and energy-boosting carbohydrates.

WHEAT

Wheat is a valuable source of essential nutrients, providing complex carbohydrates for sustained energy. It contains protein, which supports muscle growth and repair. Wheat is rich in dietary fiber, aiding digestion and promoting heart health. It also supplies important vitamins such as B vitamins (e.g., B1, B3, B5) that help in energy metabolism and cognitive function. Additionally, wheat provides minerals like iron and magnesium, which are crucial for oxygen transport and bone health.

FRUIT

Fruits are rich in vitamins, especially vitamin C, which supports immune function. They provide essential minerals like potassium and magnesium, which aid in heart and muscle health. High in dietary fiber, fruits promote healthy digestion and prevent constipation. They also contain antioxidants that protect cells from damage and reduce inflammation. Additionally, fruits offer natural sugars for quick energy while maintaining overall nutritional balance.
